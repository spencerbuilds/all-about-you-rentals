Who We Are

All About You Rentals was born from a love of making moments extraordinary. Christina Stewart — devoted mother, avid baker, and HGTV enthusiast — built this business around her innate talent for refinishing and rejuvenating vintage furniture into breathtaking décor.

What started as a small collection of hand-curated pieces has grown into a premier event rental company serving couples and families across Utah. With over 3,500 events completed and more than 200 five-star Google reviews, Christina's dedication to her clients speaks for itself.

"My goal is simple: to help you make your vision a reality."

Whether you're planning an intimate backyard gathering or a grand ballroom wedding, Christina works closely with each client to understand their vision and help bring it to life — one beautiful piece at a time.
